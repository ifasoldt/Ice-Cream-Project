Dairy Castle

"Greencastle's Premier Ice-Cream Shop"

HISTORY

Founded in 1942 by Fred Knight, Dairy Castle quickly became a popular spot for locals hankering after good home-made ice cream, DePauw students trying to impress their dates, and passerby of all sorts. Later, in 1989, Mr. Knight sold the business to its current owner Rob Zidja, who has maintained its low prices and traditional appeal.
